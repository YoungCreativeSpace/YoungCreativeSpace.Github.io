We Are the Jacuzzi Boys

A band of former collegiate distance runners took their infatuation with endurance athletics and transformed it from a recreational activity into something with conviction. Sure, we're committed to each other, but we're also committed to ourselves: to giving our potentially aimless ambitions a pointed, focused direction, while not waiting for all the stars to perfectly align only to be blinded by the sun. It's from these ideals - celebrating the process and sharing the experience with great friends - that the Jacuzzi Boys Athletic Club was born.
