Coach J-Mar



Portland-raised, Columbia-educated, and subtly vegan, Jonathan Marcus has established himself as one of the premier endurance aficionados in the PNW. When not running the show on the distance side of things at Portland State University, J-Mar can be spotted organizing the world-renowned USATF High Performance Distance Classic, throwing a number of Portland-area running events, coaching a stable of local elite athletes, or lending his expertise to the fellas of the JBAC.
